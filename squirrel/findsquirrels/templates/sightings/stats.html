Statistical Analysis of Squirrels


In the project, we work on keeping track of 3023 known squirrels in the Central Park. The data is acquired from 2018 Central Park Squirrel Census, including location coordinates, age, primary and secondary fur color, elevation, activities, communications, and interactions between squirrels and with humans.
